This paper developed a novel misalignment angle correction method for an image-based tracking continuously scanning laser Doppler vibrometer (CSLDV) for tracking and scanning a rotating structure with a large misalignment angle between its rotation axis and the optical axis of the camera in the tracking CSLDV.
