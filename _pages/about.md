I am an Assistant Professor in the Michael W. Hall School of Mechanical Engineering at Mississippi State University.

My research focuses on vibration measurement, structural dynamics, robotic laser Doppler vibrometry, continuously scanning laser Doppler vibrometry, and intelligent structural health monitoring.

Before joining Mississippi State University, I received my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Maryland, Baltimore County.
