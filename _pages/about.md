Hello! I am a tenure-track assistant professor of Mechanical Engineering at Mississippi State University (MSU) starting in Fall 2025. Before coming to MSU, I served as a Postdoc Fellow at the University of Maryland Baltimore County (UMBC). I earned my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from UMBC in 2024, guided by Dr. Weidong Zhu. Before that, I obtained my M.S. degree in Mechanical Engineering from UMBC in 2022, M.S. degree in Civil Engineering from Hunan University in 2019, and B.S. degree in Engineering Mechanics from the Zhengzhou University in 2015.

My research focuses on vibration measurement, structural dynamics, robotic laser Doppler vibrometry, continuously scanning laser Doppler vibrometry, and intelligent structural health monitoring.
